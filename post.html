How to enter in the story

Like a RPG

Come to the storyyyy

With the donation of five dollars,

  • you have the right to choose the name of a character
  • the race (Elf, dwarf, human, orc or pig man)
  • alignment (Good or bad)
  • distribute 4 points between Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence and Charisma.
  • Your character will appear in at least 4 squares.

With the donation of fifteen dollars,

  • you have the right to choose the name of a character
  • the race (Elf, dwarf, human, goblin, orc or dog-man)
  • alignment (Good or bad, background)
  • distribute 6 points between Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence and Charisma.
  • your character will appear in at least 15 squares. (spread over more than one chapter)
  • you can choose five words that you can the character speak
  • you can also choose the look of him.

With the donation of twenty five dollars,

  • you have the right to choose the name of a character
  • the race (Elf, dwarf, human, goblin, troll, orc or dog-man)
  • alignment (Good or bad, background)
  • distribute 8 points between Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence and Charisma.
  • will be a sidekick of the story
  • eventually you can choose to speak for your character
  • you can also choose the look of him.
